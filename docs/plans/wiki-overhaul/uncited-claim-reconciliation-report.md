## Reconciliation rules applied

1. When a factual paragraph belonged on a canonical page, it was preserved there and competing duplicate pages were removed or absorbed.
2. When wording sounded stronger than the support justified, the prose was tightened to a directly supported claim instead of preserving weak generalizations.
3. When source inventories were still needed on advanced technical pages, they were relabeled as implementation evidence or archive support so navigation and proof no longer used the same heading.
4. Preserved source artifacts and official mirrors were exempt from rewriting, but modern companion pages now carry the routing and synthesis burden around them.

## Final assessment

The overhaul closed the claim-verification work promised by the plan for the live 62-page wiki. Optional future enhancements remain possible, but no remaining citation or uncited-claim debt blocks completion of the consolidation and restructuring promised by the plan.
